Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response after submitting?

Our production lead typically responds within 4 business hours. For urgent inquiries on active productions, use the priority line: +91 98765 43210.

Can I request a site survey before committing?

Absolutely. We offer complimentary site surveys for events exceeding 500 guests. Our technical team will assess acoustics, power requirements, and logistics within 48 hours of your request.

Do you handle permits and regulatory compliance?

Yes. Our site logistics team manages all permits, noise ordinances, crowd control approval, and fire safety compliance on your behalf — domestically and internationally.

What's the minimum notice period to book Pulse?

Intimate events require 3–4 weeks. For festival or stadium productions, we recommend a lead time of 6–9 months to guarantee the highest quality standard and crew availability.
