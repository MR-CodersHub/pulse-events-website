FAQ Center

Your Direct Line to Production Clarity.

What audio hardware do you deploy for festivals?

We primarily deploy L-Acoustics K1/K2 and d&b audiotechnik GSL systems, ensuring world-class sonic pressure and clarity across all frequencies.

Can you handle 4K pixel-mapped LED environments?

Yes, our media server team uses Disguise and Resolume to handle massive-scale 4K and 8K LED content delivery with zero latency.

Do you handle international events?

Absolutely. We have an international logistics team capable of deploying world-class production anywhere on the globe.

What is your standard deposit structure?

We require a 30% technical deployment deposit at signing, 40% thirty days prior to the event, and the final 30% upon site handover.

What is your cancellation policy for large-scale events?

Cancellations made 60+ days out receive a 50% refund of the deposit. Inside 60 days, deposits are non-refundable due to logistics and crew allocation.

What is the minimum notice period to book Pulse?

Intimate events require 3–4 weeks. For festival or stadium productions we recommend a lead time of 6–9 months to guarantee the highest quality standard.

Do you provide on-site safety officers?

Every Pulse production includes a lead safety inspector and crowd management supervisor to ensure all builds meet international safety standards.

Do you handle permits and regulatory compliance?

Yes. Our site logistics team manages all permits, noise ordinances, crowd control approval, and fire safety compliance on your behalf — domestically and internationally.

Can you source specific international talent?

Yes, our artist relations team has direct pipelines to major talent agencies worldwide and can fulfil both emerging and headline artist requirements.

What concierge services do you offer artists?

We offer full rider fulfilment, private transport coordination, hotel and hospitality management, and dedicated on-site artist liaisons throughout the event.

Still have questions?

Our technical production desk is available around the clock. Reach out and we'll respond within 4 hours.
