Production & Specs

What audio hardware do you deploy for festivals?

We primarily deploy L-Acoustics K1/K2 and d&b audiotechnik GSL systems, ensuring world-class sonic pressure and clarity across all frequencies.

Can you handle 4K pixel-mapped LED environments?

Yes, our media server team uses Disguise and Resolume to handle massive-scale 4K and 8K LED content delivery with zero latency.

Booking & Deposits

What is your standard deposit structure?

We require a 30% technical deployment deposit at signing, 40% 30 days prior to the event, and the final 30% upon site handover.

What is your cancellation policy for large-scale events?

Cancellations made 60+ days out receive a 50% refund of the deposit. Inside 60 days, deposits are non-refundable due to logistics and crew allocation.

Logistics & Safety

Do you provide on-site safety officers?

Every Pulse production includes a lead safety inspector and crowd management supervisor to ensure all builds meet international safety standards.
