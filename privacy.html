Privacy Policy

Data Security & Production Ethics.

Last Updated: February 02, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and corporate data necessary for elite production services, including technical event specs, artist riders, and secure payment information for booking deposits.

2. Use of Production Data

Site surveys, CAD blueprints, and attendee statistics are used exclusively for optimizing sound pressure levels, lighting choreography, and crowd safety during your live event.

3. Data Protection

PulseEvents uses enterprise-grade encryption for all client communications and technical documents. We never share artist riders or corporate specifications with third parties outside our core production network.

4. International Transfers

As a global production house, your event specs may be relayed to international site surveyors. We ensure all partners adhere to the highest data production standards.
